Peace, like war, must be waged.

-George Clooney

We live in a world in which stories of death and destruction find no place in daily conversation, obscured by pop culture that is easier on the heart and mind. It isn't because we don't care but rather because we don't completely understand that behind every body count, lies a life, that behind every suicide bomber, lies a belief and that behind every war, lies an ambition.

At the TM-MUN, we believe that an education can lead to an empowerment that allows us to understand the world we live in. With understanding comes caring. And change will inevitably follow.

Join us on the 27th and 28th of September as we simulate a Model United Nations for the problems of the real world in the 4th edition of the conference, organised by the Management Society of the Thadomal Shahani Engineering College and the MUN Society of the Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management amp Engineering. In continuum with a longstanding tradition of experiencing and nurturing stimulating debates to ignite the mind and soul, the TM-MUN will see the highest calibre of Executive Board and International Press members from across the nation to bring you the world with the issues that continue to plague it. From presidents to prime ministers, the world's most powerful men and women have failed to bring peace to our world in chaos.
Will you?
